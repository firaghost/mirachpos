# MirachPOS Privacy Policy

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-02-04  

---

## 1. Who We Are

MirachPOS provides point-of-sale software for cafes and restaurants in Ethiopia.  
**Contact:** privacy@mirachpos.com  

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

### From Account Owners
- Name, email, phone
- Business name, address
- Login credentials (password hashed with bcrypt)

### From Staff
- Name, email, phone
- Role (Cashier, Manager, etc.)
- PIN code (hashed)
- Shift logs (clock in/out times)

### Business Data You Enter
- Orders, sales transactions
- Inventory items
- Menu items and prices
- Customer records (if you add them)
- Supplier information

### Automatically Collected
- IP address
- Device type and browser
- Access times and pages visited
- Error logs

### Payment Data
**We do NOT store:**
- Full credit card numbers
- CVV codes
- Card expiration dates

**We store:**
- Transaction reference IDs from Chapa/Telebirr
- Last 4 digits of card (if provided by gateway)
- Payment amount and status

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Provide POS service | Orders, inventory, staff data |
| Process payments | Transaction references |
| Send receipts/invoices | Email addresses |
| Security | IP addresses, login times |
| Support | Account info, error logs |
| Billing | Subscription status, invoices |

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Access your sales data for analytics (unless you opt in)

## 6. Data Storage

**Location:** AWS servers (may include EU regions for redundancy)

**Retention:**
| Data Type | Kept For |
|-----------|----------|
| Account data | Duration of account + 2 years |
| Transactions | 7 years (tax law) |
| Audit logs | 1 year |
| Deleted accounts | 30 days (then purged) |

---

## 7. Security Measures

- TLS encryption for all connections
- Passwords hashed with bcrypt
- Database access restricted
- Regular security updates
- No raw card data stored (PCI SAQ-A)

---

## 8. Your Rights

### Access
Request a copy of your data: privacy@mirachpos.com

### Deletion
Delete your account in settings or email us. Data purged within 90 days.

### Correction
Update info in your profile or contact support.

### Portability
Export your data as JSON from the dashboard.

### Withdraw Consent
Unsubscribe from emails or email privacy@mirachpos.com
